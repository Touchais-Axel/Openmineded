One Piece, the genius of a man

One Piece is the story of a boy who wants to become the king of the pirates. Since 1997, this story has captivated and fascinated everyone. The manga reached 500 million copies in July 2022 and became the best-selling comic book series by a single author in the world. The work surpasses Batman and Spider-Man in particular!

A fan's hard work for the fans

Oda, the author of the manga, has to submit a 19-page chapter every week! Of course, it is impossible to do everything alone. So he is surrounded by assistants who advise him and help him in his tedious work...

But when it comes to the script, it's him, body and soul. "If I am not moved myself, I do not believe I can move others. The mangaka is particularly keen to draw the characters and the central elements of his plates himself.

Because of the rhythm imposed by his work, the author has a deplorable life hygiene. If he continues despite everything, it is, he says: "because I think it is impolite not to draw episodes that I find interesting."

A break? Not really

When he took a four-week break from his weekly publication earlier this summer, the event set the media ablaze. And for good reason: it's rare. Nevertheless, it's a mistake to think that he was on vacation.

He first devoted this break to fine-tune the launch of the movie "One Piece Red", which will be released on August 6 in Japan (the 10th in France). Finally, he also took the opportunity to work and rethink the final part of the manga.

An end that he had already imagined since the beginning of the work. But he wanted to readjust it so that it would be up to the fans' expectations. As what, even when he doesn't work directly on the work, the mangaka doesn't take rest for all that!

When you see the hard work of an author like Eiichiro Oda, you think that it is something unique. But imagine that one day artificial intelligence will replace your favorite artist. This is what will surely happen!
