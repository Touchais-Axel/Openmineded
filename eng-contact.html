Between you and me

Who am I?

I am Axel Touchais and I am 20 years old. I'm a student in my first year of BUT, Multimedia and Internet professions in Montbéliard. I created this website from A to Z for my first semester individual project. The exercise consisted in creating a website, optimized for iPhoneX, on a rather free subject: writing for our future. We had to take notes for later, about a place, a music, a movie. The goal was to leave a trace, which will touch us in a few years, but which will also touch people whose interests converge with ours.
