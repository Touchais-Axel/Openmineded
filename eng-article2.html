Nepal, the angel of death

Nepal is an artist who made a huge impression on me. He was very little known, and this because he never showed his face. The Parisian rapper wanted people to appreciate him for his music, not for his appearance. I attach a special importance to "Malik Al Mawt", and in this article, I explain you what makes this song special.

A fighter's vision

Nepal used to share his thoughts through his music, which created a real connection with the people who listened to him. This is the case with the song "Malik Al Mawt", which means "the angel of death" in Arabic.

The rapper sings a single verse, which sounds like a message of struggle against the system. You really get the feeling that he is writing what he is thinking at the moment, which gives authenticity to his music.

Nepal refers in particular to the silent majority of the population who remain silent and thus avoid problems. He then explains that he chose not to be part of this majority, and thus to express himself freely.

In the following measures, Nepal talks about his vision of death, which he sees as the end of a path. No matter what happens to him, no matter what is good or bad, after death only the soul remains.

An immersive universe

If "Malik Al Mawt" is one of my favorite pieces of music from Nepal, it is also because the structure of the music puts the listener in a real atmosphere.

The song as a whole, whether it is the lyrics or the instrumental, is simple but really pleasant to listen to. It lasts just over two minutes, which does not give us time to get bored. This makes it an impactful music.

Nepal made most of his instrumentals himself, showing a creative intelligence, especially for the use of samples. The guitar for example, is present throughout the music. Its melody is soft and soothes us. The rapper lets us enjoy it by letting the instrumental continue freely at the end of his verse, which allows us to enjoy it until the very last second.

All these elements participate in the setting up of an immersive universe, which brings us a little closer to Nepal and its thoughts.

In 2019, unfortunately, the artist left us. The same year, the rapper Nekfeu released his documentary "Les Étoiles Vagabondes". Nepal participated in this journey, he who was part of the close entourage of the rapper.
